## Users

Two intertwined audiences served by a single home that branches.

**Path A: Heart-led healing seekers.** Mostly women 30–55, spiritual-curious, often empaths or sensitives, searching for energy healing, sound healing, Reiki, or chakra and aura clearing. They book virtual sessions worldwide or in-person sessions in Charlotte, North Carolina and Kathmandu Valley. Their context is private, often after a hard chapter: grief, burnout, relationship transition, the quiet feeling that something is off. They need safety, evidence Christina is real, and a gentle path to the booking page.

**Path B: Nepal pilgrims and conscious travelers.** People comparing transformational retreat hosts and Himalayan guides: yoga teachers leading groups, solo travelers researching Nepal retreats, couples planning a sacred-region trip, sensitives who want a guided pilgrimage rather than an itinerary. They want immersive storytelling, itinerary substance, and trust that the host genuinely lives the place. Their conversion is a private inquiry, an application, or a retreat hold.

The home page splits these paths cleanly so neither feels like an afterthought.

## Product Purpose

Tune Vibration is the website of Christina Landry — Energeticist, Energy Alchemist, sound healer, Reiki practitioner, intuitive guide, and Nepal retreat host. Born in Charlotte, North Carolina and now rooted in the Kathmandu Valley and Lalitpur, Nepal, the brand has evolved from a local healing practice into a Nepal-based hub offering virtual and in-person healing sessions, multi-day retreats, retreat housing, Himalayan treks, and Kathmandu Valley local guide services.

The site exists to:

1. Enchant the visitor into slowing down — the first thirty seconds set the nervous system, the rest of the site benefits from it.
2. Make Christina knowable: voice, face, lineage, Charlotte roots, Nepal practice, what she actually does in a session.
3. Present services so a sensitive person can self-select without comparison-shopping or sales pressure.
4. Open a believable door into Nepal — the retreats, the treks, the valley tours, the housing — as an extension of the same practice, not a side hustle.
5. Convert quietly: book a session, hold a retreat date, request a private guide, or open a conversation.

Success looks like a visitor who lingers, exhales, and either books, applies, or opens a conversation feeling met rather than sold.

## Brand Personality

Three words: **Mystic. Warm. Grounded.**

- **Mystic** — the site implies more than it states. Negative space, unfinished sentences, ink that bleeds, motion that breathes. The viewer feels something they cannot quite name.
- **Warm** — sandstone, rice-paper, candle-amber. Christina is not a remote high-priestess; she is a wise, present, available human. Copy lands close, never above.
- **Grounded** — Nepal is real geography, Charlotte is real geography, Christina is a real practitioner with a real lineage. Mysticism without flake. Earthiness underneath every ethereal flourish.

The voice is intimate, plain, occasionally lyrical. Spoken aloud, it sounds like someone you trust speaking softly. Never breathless. Never salesy. Never academic.

## Anti-References

The site explicitly avoids the visual languages of these categories. They are the four reflex traps for spiritual brands; recognize and reject.

1. **Wellness-template look.** Squarespace earthy beige, photo-of-hands-on-back, identical service cards, generic "self-care" stock imagery, sage-and-eucalyptus palette. Reads template, not human.
2. **New-age clip-art.** Rainbow chakra wheels, mandala overlays, lotus-everywhere, sparkle particles, glowing third-eye decals. Reads kitsch, undermines trust.
3. **Crystal-shop e-commerce.** Rose-gold gradients, glitter, mystical script fonts (Sacramento, Allura, Great Vibes), Etsy-shop layouts. Reads merchandise.
4. **Influencer-coach landing pages.** Countdown timers, before-and-after testimonials, money-promise heroes, pop-up offers, urgency tactics, $$$ framing. Reads transactional and contradicts the brand premise.

Adjacent traps to avoid: generic AI design patterns (identical icon-and-text card grids, hero-metric templates, glassmorphism, gradient text), gold/navy luxury cliché borrowed from real-estate templates, "modern minimalism" that strips the soul out and leaves Helvetica on white.

## Design Principles

1. **The first thirty seconds is a breath.** The home opens slow on purpose. Motion is paced to a calm exhale. Visitors who do not slow down are not the audience.
2. **Show the place, not symbols of the place.** Real Nepal imagery, real session photography, real handwritten artifacts. No mandala stock art, no lotus overlays, no chakra rainbows. Geography and material do the spiritual work.
3. **Every page has one signature moment.** A single bespoke gesture rooted in that page's topic — a bowl wave, a prayer-flag drift, a topographic contour reveal, a candle-bokeh testimonial. Same token system, distinct soul per surface.
4. **Restraint signals authority.** Whitespace, silence, and one held color do more than five accents. Christina's confidence in her work is mirrored in the design's confidence in the work it doesn't do.
5. **Two paths, one home.** Healing and Nepal travel are first-class siblings, not a primary and a sub-page. The home composition holds both without forcing a hierarchy.
6. **Gentle is engineered.** Soft is not lazy. Reaching gentle requires precision — type pairings tuned to the millimeter, motion eased to a millisecond, color tinted off true neutral. Easy on the eye is hard on the engineer.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- **WCAG 2.2 AA across the site.** Body text ≥ 4.5:1, large display ≥ 3:1, focus rings always visible at ≥ 3:1 against background.
- **Strict reduced-motion mode.** When `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` is set, scroll-linked motion, parallax, drift, and ink-bloom reveals are disabled entirely (not just shortened). Static composition replaces motion narrative. Migraine and vestibular sensitivities are a real subset of the spiritual-seeker audience and treated as first-class.
- **Sensory-considerate motion at baseline.** Even with motion enabled, no rapid flashes (≤ 3Hz), no parallax that decouples camera from scroll, no auto-playing audio without an obvious mute.
- **Touch targets ≥ 44px** on all interactive elements. Form fields ≥ 48px high. Booking and contact flows readable at 200% zoom.
- **Keyboard navigability** verified on every interactive surface. Skip-link present.
- **Alt text** that describes the spiritual or geographic context, not just the literal subject ("Christina holding a Tibetan bowl mid-tone" not "woman with bowl").
