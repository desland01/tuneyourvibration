## A note from Christina

I built Tune Your Vibration after my own nervous system crash taught me that sensitivity is not a flaw—it is a compass. When we let the body speak through sound and sensation, life reorganises around clarity, creativity, and calm. My role is to hold a regulated field so you can soften, receive, and remember the soul-level reasons you are here.

## Outcomes clients share most often

- Deeper sleep and a noticeable drop in recurring anxiety loops.
- Rekindled creative energy that had been on pause since burnout or loss.
- Courage to set gentle but firm boundaries with family, work, and community.
- A felt sense of reunion with their body after months—or years—of dissociation.
