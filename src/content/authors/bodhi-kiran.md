Bodhi Kiran journals the dreams, whispers, and energetic visions that emerge from Christina’s ceremonies. Each poem captures the textures of integration—how a single bowl strike can unlock memories, emotions, and brand-new timelines. His work invites clients to keep listening long after the sound fades.
