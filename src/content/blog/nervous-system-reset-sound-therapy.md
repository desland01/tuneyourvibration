## Why sound resets your nervous system

Sound therapy works through resonance. When crystal bowls, tuning forks, and my channeled voice hold steady frequencies, your body entrains to them. That matters because:

- **Vibrations cue the vagus nerve** to shift you from fight-or-flight into rest-and-digest. Clients often feel their breath drop lower within minutes.
- **Layered tones unwind muscular guarding.** Just like a weighted blanket, harmonic intervals send your fascia the signal that it can soften.
- **Brainwave states downshift.** Binaural beat patterns and singing bowls invite alpha and theta waves, which restore creativity, emotional regulation, and deep sleep.
