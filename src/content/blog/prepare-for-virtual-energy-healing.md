Use this guide to turn any room into a sacred container before your next online ceremony.

## Anchor your intention before the call

Five minutes of intention work clears digital noise and invites your guides to co-create the session.

- **Name the shift you crave.** Say out loud, “I’m ready to release launch fatigue and welcome rested confidence,” or whatever transformation feels alive today.
- **Choose an anchor object.** Select a crystal, journal, or photograph that represents the version of you emerging through the session.
- **Signal your availability.** Silence notifications, close browsers, and place your phone in airplane mode. This tells your energy body it is safe to soften.

## Build a sensory field that supports nervous system calm

Christina’s virtual energy healing sessions stream studio-grade audio, so amplify the effect with supportive sensory cues.

- **Lighting:** Soften overhead lights, add warm lamps, or use candlelight so your body drops into parasympathetic rest quickly.
- **Temperature:** Layer blankets or a weighted throw; distance energy healing often lowers the body’s temperature as it unwinds.
- **Scent:** Diffuse a single plant ally—lavender, palo santo, or rose—rather than a strong blend. Subtlety keeps your awareness inside the session rather than on the aroma.

## Set up ergonomic comfort even if you’ll be lying down

Remote healing works best when your body feels supported and alert-free.

- **Headphones or speakers:** High-fidelity headphones reproduce Christina’s crystal bowls accurately, while a small Bluetooth speaker can keep the breath cues spatial.
- **Support props:** Stack pillows under your knees, neck, or wrists if you’re lying on a mat. Sitting? Use a chair with lumbar support and add a cushion under your feet.
- **Hydration:** Fill a glass bottle ahead of time. Water becomes an energetic conductor once the frequencies start moving.

## Ground your tech for a glitch-free ceremony

Interruptions break the energetic flow. Reduce them with quick tech hygiene.

- Restart your laptop or tablet earlier in the day so pending updates don’t hijack the session.
- Close heavy applications, then open your meeting link in a single browser tab.
- Plug devices into power, and have a backup hotspot ready if your Wi-Fi wobbles.
- Position your camera slightly above eye level if Christina will be tracking breath or posture.

## Invite your body to collaborate with the energy

Distance Reiki healer sessions are co-creative. Let your physical body know it’s part of the team.

- Take three slow belly breaths while gently touching your heart and solar plexus. This tells the vagus nerve a safe guide is present.
- Stretch your spine, roll your shoulders, shake out wrists and ankles, and drink a few sips of water.
- Keep a journal or voice memo app nearby so insights can land before you re-enter daily life.

## Close with integration time after the session

Give yourself at least 20 minutes post-call to metabolize what moved. Journal prompts you can try:

1. What sensations did I feel as the remote energy healing unfolded?
2. Which messages or images arrived, and how can I honor them this week?
3. What boundary, ritual, or action will keep this new frequency anchored?

Light movement, a nourishing snack, or grounding barefoot in your yard also help your aura seal.
