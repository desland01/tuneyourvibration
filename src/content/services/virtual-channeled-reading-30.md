## When to book the short reading

- You already sense the answer and want confirmation from a trusted intuitive guide.
- A decision window is approaching and you need clarity without a full deep-dive.
- You recently had a longer session and want to calibrate how the energy is unfolding.

## Simple prep ritual

1. Breathe deeply for three cycles while holding your question in mind.
2. Silence notifications so the call can stay laser-focused.
3. Keep a notebook or note-taking app nearby for any phrases that spark action.

## Keep the momentum

- Expect a post-session voice note summarizing the key themes so you can replay as needed.
- Anchor the guidance with the suggested affirmation or ritual.
- If more questions surface, upgrade to the 60-minute reading for wider exploration.
